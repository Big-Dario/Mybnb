About AirStay

Kenya's premier property booking platform

Our Mission

AirStay connects travellers with Kenya's finest homes, villas, and apartments. We believe every journey deserves a beautiful place to rest — whether it's a beachfront villa in Diani or a cosy apartment in Nairobi.

Founded in 2022, we've helped over 48,000 guests find their perfect stay across 32 cities in Kenya and East Africa.

Why Choose AirStay?

  • ✓ All properties personally verified by our team
  • ✓ Secure payments via M-Pesa, card or bank transfer
  • ✓ 24/7 guest support team based in Nairobi
  • ✓ Free cancellation on most properties
  • ✓ Best price guarantee
